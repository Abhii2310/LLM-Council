# LLM Council Knowledge Base

## Renewable Energy Basics
Renewable energy includes solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and biomass. The key benefit is lower greenhouse-gas emissions compared to fossil fuels. Key challenges include intermittency, grid integration, and storage costs.

## Electric Vehicle Benefits
Electric vehicles can reduce tailpipe emissions, lower operating costs, and reduce oil dependence. Their overall environmental impact depends on electricity mix, battery manufacturing, and lifecycle recycling.

## AI Future Trends
AI systems are moving toward multimodal reasoning, retrieval-augmented generation, and agentic workflows. Governance priorities include model safety, transparency, and measurable evaluation benchmarks.

## RAG Guidelines
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combines a retriever with a generator. A practical pipeline is: ingest documents, chunk text, index chunks, retrieve top-k relevant chunks at query time, and then generate grounded answers with citations.

## Multi-LLM Evaluation
For a council-style architecture, compare answers by factuality, latency, clarity, and source grounding. Aggregate with confidence weighting and keep traceability to the original sources.
